The Barn

The big doors groan open into warm lantern light, polished rails, name plates, fresh straw, and the soft restless sounds of Celina's prettiest farm trophies. This is where visitors come to look, admire, compare notes, and decide whether they are brave enough to step closer than the fence.

Show Animals

This row is set up like the item collection on the main page: each animal gets a portrait, a clear role, a little lore, and a few quick traits. These are placeholders for now, but the layout is ready for real barn residents, transformation endings, unlock states, or custom pages.

Velvet

Show Dog - former visitor

Velvet is kept nearest to the entrance because she loves attention. She perks up whenever boots hit the floorboards, eager to show how quickly pride can become obedience when the barn decides someone belongs on all fours.

Brindle

Pack Donkey - stubborn prize

Brindle still tries to look unimpressed whenever someone reads the brass plate on his stall. The act never lasts. One scratch under the chin and the former loudmouth remembers exactly why Celina keeps him hauling baskets for the rest of the barn.

Rosethorn

Show Horse - ribbon candidate

Rosethorn gets the center stall, groomed glossy and displayed beneath the best lantern. Every ribbon on the wall is a reminder that Celina does not just collect animals. She curates them, trains them, and shows them off when they are finally worth looking at.

Vesper's Side Table


The blackjack table is no longer sitting loose on the farm map. It belongs here, tucked behind the animal showcase where the lanterns get lower, the chip tray gleams, and the barn's claimed residents become part warning sign and part prize display.

Vesper keeps the rules simple: win hands, earn chips, reveal the ledger, and peek at the poor souls already marked by the house. Lose too hard and the table does not just take your wager: it chooses a new place for you in the barn, as a pet, a show animal, or one of Celina's useful belongings. The game can stay here as a contained feature while the wider farm connection waits until it is fully finished.


Win: collect chips, reveal claimed losers, and buy your way deeper into Vesper's private little game.
Lose: the barn marks you, reshapes you, and files you away as something Celina can keep or show off.
