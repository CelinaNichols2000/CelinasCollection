Velvet

Show Dog

Description

Velvet is one of the first animals visitors notice when they enter the barn. She is kept close to the front rail, brushed soft, collared neatly, and trained to perk up whenever footsteps cross the straw.

She used to be far too proud for simple obedience. Now she waits for attention with bright eyes, wagging whenever someone reads her name plate and realizes the barn does not only keep ordinary livestock.
