Description

Brindle is the barn's stubborn placeholder prize, kept near the hayloft where everyone can see the result of mouthing off after the magic has already won.

He still carries himself like he is above the whole arrangement, but the baskets, tack, and little brass name plate tell a different story. The barn has a way of making usefulness stick.
