#1: SCARLET'S TRANSFORMATION
I WON'T! Scarlet's magic slams into me—body compressing, shrinking down. My ears stretch LONG, fuzzy, swiveling wildly. STOP THIS! But she just smirks, circling with her riding crop. "Going to make you the most stubborn little work donkey in my stable." My spine cracks—forcing me toward all fours. Gray fur erupts across my skin.
#2: ASININE FEATURES
Tried to protest—came out as a harsh bray. HEE-HAW! No! That's not MY voice! Hands harden into hooves—I crash onto all fours. A tufted tail sprouts from my rear. I'M STILL ME! But Scarlet slaps my flank hard. "Stubborn beasts need to learn their place. You'll pull my carts whether you like it or not." She buckles a harness onto my shrinking frame.
#3: STUBBORN COMPLIANCE
Mind at WAR with itself. I DON'T WANT TO OBEY! But when Scarlet says "Pull," my legs move. When she says "Stop," I freeze. Body betrays... stubborn mind... can't resist... She hooks me to a heavy cart. I plant my hooves, refusing—but she cracks her whip. "Move, beast." My legs pull anyway. Hate this. But... pulling. Working. Obeying.
#4: MINDLESS WORKER
Awareness fading. Just simple beast now. Pull cart. Carry load. Obey master. Scarlet rides on my back sometimes, heels digging into my flanks. I trot obediently despite mental resistance. "Such a good work donkey. Stubborn, but you always obey in the end." She's right. I bray irritably but my body serves perfectly. This is life now. Work. Serve. Exist as beast.
#5: PERMANENT SERVITUDE
HEE-HAW! Work donkey! Master's beast! Pull forever!
Human memories completely gone. Just donkey instincts remain. Grass good. Water refreshing. Harness familiar. Carts normal. Work is life. Scarlet brushes my coat sometimes—I lean into her touch despite still feeling stubborn. "My perfect little worker. Always resisting, always obeying anyway." She's right. I bray irritably when she loads heavy carts. But I pull them perfectly every time. This is existence. Stubborn beast. Obedient donkey. Serve master. Forever.
