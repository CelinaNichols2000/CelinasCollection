Under The Table

You shrank inside your clothes while Vesper held up the leash for the next player to admire. She did not drag you away. She kept you close.

Now the underside of the blackjack table is your view, and every chip dropped near your paws reminds you why dogs should not gamble.

She uses you as part of the show. Before a risky hand, she rests one heel on the leash and tells the player what you wagered, how confident you looked, and how quickly confidence turned into panting.

Sometimes she drops a chip just to watch you nose it back. Sometimes she calls you by the amount you lost. Either way your tail wags before your pride can stop it, and Vesper notices every single time.
