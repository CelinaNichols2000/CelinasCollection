The Stables

The smell hits before the door even closes — hay, leather, and something underneath that prickles at the back of your neck. The stalls run the full length of both walls. Something in one shifts at the sound of your footsteps. It doesn't sound quite like a horse.

Six women. Each with her own section. Each already aware you walked in. None of them are going to let you browse.

EXTREME
Vex Harlow
Head Groom · 22

Leather gloves. Riding crop that never stops moving. She read you the moment you walked in and already wrote you down in the horse column. She hasn't been wrong yet.

EXTREME
Nora Ash
Breed Witch · 25

Warm, patient, persuasive. She speaks like someone who already knows the ending. She's been certain about you since you crossed the threshold and she's never had to revise that certainty.

HIGH
Thessaly
Wild Witch · 19

Nineteen and genuinely delighted by everything that's about to happen to you. The enthusiasm is the most dangerous thing about her. She is very, very enthusiastic.

HIGH
Briar Cole
Cowgirl · 24

Southern drawl, golden lasso at her hip, and a very unsettling comfort around livestock. She keeps looking at you like she's measuring where to put the nameplate.

EXTREME
Lena Drake
Tack Alchemist · 21

She makes the tack — bridles, saddles, crops. She has a compound for each one. She has been looking at your skin the way a tanner looks at a very promising hide.

HIGH
Petra
Pony Trainer · 20

The sweetest smile in the stables. The most careful hands. She is genuinely, warmly proud of everything she has ever done to everyone who walked into her ring.
